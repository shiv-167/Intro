About Me

Hello! I'm Shiv — a passionate web designer and developer with a background in civil engineering. I specialize in creating clean, functional, and visually engaging websites that not only look great but also deliver a smooth user experience. My goal is to help individuals and businesses bring their ideas to life through modern and responsive web design.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from Tribhuvan University, where I developed a strong foundation in technical problem-solving, structural design, and analytical thinking. Currently, I’m working at the Department of Roads, Nepal, where I contribute to infrastructure development that shapes our nation's connectivity and growth.

While my professional background lies in civil engineering, my passion for technology and design led me to the world of web development. Now, I combine the precision of engineering with the creativity of design to build meaningful digital experiences.

Feel free to explore my Web-Apps to see some of the projects I've worked on — from structural design tools to sleek websites. Let’s connect and build something amazing together!
